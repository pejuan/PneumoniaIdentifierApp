About Pneumonia identifier

The Pneumonia identifier app was a project developed as part of José Pejuán's MSc Big Data Analytics dissertation for University of Liverpool.

It utilizes machine learning algorithms to diagnose pneumonia on chest x-ray images. To protect your privacy, none of the images uploaded are stored.

The application can diagnose pneumonia in children and adults. Nevertheless, it works best on children 5 years old or younger.

The purpose of the application is to aid in identification of pneumonia and not as a substitute for medical attention. Predictions have a margin of error and that is why a confidence score is included in the results.
